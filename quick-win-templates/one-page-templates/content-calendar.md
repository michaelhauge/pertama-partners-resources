## Content Workflow

**Ideation** (Week -4):
- [ ] Brainstorm topics (team meeting, 1 hour)
- [ ] Research keywords (SEO tool: Ahrefs, SEMrush)
- [ ] Choose topics (align with goals)

**Creation** (Week -3 to -1):
- [ ] Week -3: Write drafts (writers)
- [ ] Week -2: Edit (editor)
- [ ] Week -1: Design (designer: graphics, video)
- [ ] Week 0: Schedule (marketing ops)

**Publication** (Week 0):
- [ ] Monday 8am: Blog post live
- [ ] Monday 10am: LinkedIn post
- [ ] Monday 12pm: Twitter thread
- [ ] Tuesday 9am: Email campaign
- [ ] Wednesday: Video/podcast

**Promotion** (Week 0-1):
- [ ] Reshare 3-5x on social (different angles)
- [ ] Share in communities (Reddit, forums, Slack groups)
- [ ] Paid promotion (if applicable)

**Measurement** (Week 1):
- [ ] Track performance (GA, social analytics)
- [ ] Identify top performers (what worked?)
- [ ] Iterate for next week

## Pro Tips

**1. Batch Create Content**
- Write 4 blog posts in one day (vs 1 per week)
- Record 4 videos in one session
- **Why**: Saves context-switching time

**2. Repurpose Everything**
- 1 blog post â†’ 5 LinkedIn posts + 10 tweets + 1 email + 1 video
- **Formula**: 1 core piece â†’ 15-20 micro pieces

**3. Theme Each Month**
- Makes planning easier
- Creates narrative arc
- Easier to promote ("This month we're focusing on X")

**4. Use Templates**
- Blog post template (H1, intro, body, conclusion, CTA)
- Social post template (hook, body, CTA)
- Email template (subject, preview, body, PS)
