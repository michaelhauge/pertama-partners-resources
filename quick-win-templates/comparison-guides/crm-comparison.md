## Detailed Comparison

### HubSpot CRM

**Best for**: SMBs that need marketing + sales in one platform

**Pricing** (5 users):
- **Free**: Basic CRM (unlimited users)
- **Starter**: $45/mo ($225 for 5) - Email, forms, landing pages
- **Professional**: $180/mo ($900 for 5) - Marketing automation, workflows
- **Enterprise**: $360/mo+ ($1,800+ for 5) - Advanced reporting, custom objects

**Pros**:
- ✅ Free tier is genuinely good (not crippled)
- ✅ Best marketing automation in this list
- ✅ Clean UI, easy to learn
- ✅ App marketplace (1,000+ integrations)
- ✅ Strong for inbound (blog, SEO, landing pages)

**Cons**:
- ❌ Expensive as you scale (Professional = $180/user/mo)
- ❌ Reporting limited on lower tiers
- ❌ Some features require add-ons ($$$)
- ❌ Contact limits on Starter tier (1,000 contacts)

**SEA-Specific**:
- Support: Email/chat in SG business hours (8am-6pm SGT)
- Payment: Credit card (SGD, USD, EUR)
- Data center: US/EU (no SEA data residency)
- Local integrations: Xero, Stripe (but no Xendit, Midtrans)

**When to choose**:
- You need marketing + sales in one tool
- You have inbound leads (blog, SEO, ads)
- Budget: $500-2,000/month for CRM + marketing

**Migration effort**: 2-3 weeks (medium)

### Zoho CRM

**Best for**: Budget-conscious companies that need customization

**Pricing** (5 users):
- **Standard**: $14/user/mo ($70 for 5) - Basic CRM, email, mobile
- **Professional**: $23/user/mo ($115 for 5) - Workflows, inventory, quotes
- **Enterprise**: $40/user/mo ($200 for 5) - Advanced customization, AI
- **Ultimate**: $52/user/mo ($260 for 5) - Multi-user portal, advanced BI

**Pros**:
- ✅ Cheapest in this list (by far)
- ✅ Highly customizable (custom fields, layouts, modules)
- ✅ Strong SEA presence (offices in SG, MY, ID, TH, PH)
- ✅ Multi-currency (SGD, MYR, IDR, THB, VND, PHP)
- ✅ Zoho ecosystem (One, Books, Campaigns, Desk)

**Cons**:
- ❌ UI feels dated (2015 design language)
- ❌ Steep learning curve (too many features)
- ❌ Mobile app is clunky
- ❌ Documentation is hit-or-miss

**SEA-Specific**:
- Support: 24/7 phone/email/chat in English, Bahasa, Tamil
- Payment: Credit card, bank transfer (SGD, MYR, IDR)
- Data center: India, Australia (SEA data residency optional)
- Local integrations: Xero, Zoho Books (SG/MY/ID tax compliance), Xendit

**When to choose**:
- Budget <$500/month for CRM
- You need lots of customization
- You're already using Zoho products (Books, One, Campaigns)
- You have technical admin who can configure it

**Migration effort**: 3-4 weeks (high, due to customization)

### Freshsales (by Freshworks)

**Best for**: Teams that want fast setup and clean UX

**Pricing** (5 users):
- **Free**: Basic CRM (up to 3 users)
- **Growth**: $18/user/mo ($90 for 5) - Email, phone, workflows
- **Pro**: $47/user/mo ($235 for 5) - AI scoring, multiple pipelines
- **Enterprise**: $83/user/mo ($415 for 5) - Custom reports, dedicated support

**Pros**:
- ✅ Beautiful, modern UI (best in class)
- ✅ Fast setup (1 day to go live)
- ✅ Built-in phone (VoIP calling)
- ✅ AI lead scoring on Pro tier
- ✅ Good mobile app

**Cons**:
- ❌ Less customizable than Zoho
- ❌ Reporting is basic (not as deep as Salesforce)
- ❌ Marketing features weaker than HubSpot
- ❌ Smaller app marketplace (<200 integrations)

**SEA-Specific**:
- Support: 24/7 email/chat, phone 9am-6pm SGT
- Payment: Credit card (SGD, USD)
- Data center: US, EU, India (SEA data residency via India)
- Local integrations: Limited (Stripe, Xero, but no Xendit)

**When to choose**:
- You want to be up and running in <1 week
- Clean UX is important (your team will actually use it)
- You need built-in calling (no Aircall/RingCentral integration)
- Budget: $500-1,500/month

**Migration effort**: 1-2 weeks (low)

### Pipedrive

**Best for**: Visual thinkers who love pipeline management

**Pricing** (5 users):
- **Essential**: $14/user/mo ($70 for 5) - Pipeline, email, mobile
- **Advanced**: $34/user/mo ($170 for 5) - Workflows, email sync
- **Professional**: $49/user/mo ($245 for 5) - Revenue forecasting, custom fields
- **Power**: $64/user/mo ($320 for 5) - Project tracking, permissions
- **Enterprise**: $99/user/mo ($495 for 5) - Unlimited custom fields, dedicated support

**Pros**:
- ✅ Best pipeline visualization (Kanban-style)
- ✅ Incredibly intuitive (lowest learning curve)
- ✅ Great mobile app (best for field sales)
- ✅ Activity-focused (track calls, emails, meetings)
- ✅ Sales-specific (not bloated with marketing features)

**Cons**:
- ❌ No marketing automation (email only)
- ❌ Reporting requires Professional tier
- ❌ Limited customization vs Zoho/Salesforce
- ❌ Fewer integrations than HubSpot

**SEA-Specific**:
- Support: 24/7 email/chat (English only)
- Payment: Credit card (USD, EUR, GBP - no SGD)
- Data center: US, EU (no SEA data residency)
- Local integrations: Stripe, Xero (no SEA-specific)

**When to choose**:
- Your sales process is pipeline-driven (stages, deals)
- Your team hates CRMs (Pipedrive is easiest to adopt)
- You don't need marketing features
- Budget: $300-1,000/month

**Migration effort**: 1-2 weeks (low)

### Salesforce Sales Cloud

**Best for**: Enterprise teams with complex workflows

**Pricing** (5 users):
- **Essentials**: $25/user/mo ($125 for 5) - Basic CRM (up to 10 users)
- **Professional**: $80/user/mo ($400 for 5) - Full CRM, forecasting
- **Enterprise**: $165/user/mo ($825 for 5) - Advanced customization, sandbox
- **Unlimited**: $330/user/mo ($1,650 for 5) - 24/7 support, unlimited everything

**Pros**:
- ✅ Most powerful (can do anything)
- ✅ Enterprise-grade security and compliance
- ✅ Largest app marketplace (AppExchange: 5,000+ apps)
- ✅ Best for complex sales (multiple stakeholders, long cycles)
- ✅ Strong in SEA (offices, partners, support)

**Cons**:
- ❌ Most expensive (2-3x more than alternatives)
- ❌ Requires Salesforce admin (technical expertise)
- ❌ Steep learning curve (1-2 months to get productive)
- ❌ Overkill for SMBs (<50 people)

**SEA-Specific**:
- Support: 24/7 phone/email/chat in English
- Payment: Credit card, invoice (SGD, USD)
- Data center: Singapore data center available (extra cost)
- Local integrations: Everything (Xero, Xendit, 2C2P, Midtrans, etc.)

**When to choose**:
- Enterprise sales (>$50K deal size, 6+ month cycles)
- Complex org structure (territories, hierarchies)
- You need enterprise features (SSO, SOC2, data residency)
- Budget: $2,000-10,000/month

**Migration effort**: 4-8 weeks (very high)

## Decision Framework

### Step 1: Identify Your Must-Haves

Check all that apply:

**Basic CRM**:
- [ ] Contact management (name, email, company)
- [ ] Deal pipeline (stages, amounts, close dates)
- [ ] Email integration (Gmail, Outlook)
- [ ] Task management (to-dos, reminders)
- [ ] Mobile app (iOS, Android)

**Advanced CRM**:
- [ ] Marketing automation (email campaigns, forms, landing pages)
- [ ] Workflows (if X happens, do Y)
- [ ] Custom fields (beyond name/email/company)
- [ ] Reporting (dashboards, forecasts)
- [ ] Multi-currency (SGD, MYR, IDR, etc.)

**Enterprise CRM**:
- [ ] Advanced customization (custom objects, apex code)
- [ ] Data residency (SG data center)
- [ ] SSO (single sign-on with Google/Okta)
- [ ] Role-based permissions (territories, hierarchies)
- [ ] API access (integrate with internal tools)

---

### Step 2: Set Your Budget

| Budget/Month | Recommended CRMs |
|--------------|------------------|
| <$300 | Zoho (Standard or Professional) |
| $300-800 | HubSpot (Starter), Freshsales (Growth), Pipedrive (Advanced) |
| $800-2,000 | HubSpot (Professional), Zoho (Enterprise), Freshsales (Pro) |
| $2,000+ | Salesforce (Professional or Enterprise) |

---

### Step 3: Consider Your Team

**Small team (3-10 reps)**:
- Choose: Pipedrive, Freshsales, or HubSpot Free
- Why: Simple, fast setup, low learning curve

**Mid-size team (10-30 reps)**:
- Choose: HubSpot (Pro), Zoho (Enterprise), or Salesforce (Professional)
- Why: Need workflows, reporting, customization

**Large team (30+ reps)**:
- Choose: Salesforce (Enterprise) or Zoho (Ultimate)
- Why: Need territories, hierarchies, advanced permissions

---

### Step 4: Evaluate SEA-Specific Needs

**Do you need**:
- Multi-currency? → Zoho, Salesforce
- Local payment methods? → Zoho (bank transfer in SGD/MYR/IDR)
- SEA data residency? → Salesforce (SG data center), Zoho (India/Australia)
- 24/7 support in English + local languages? → Zoho
- Integration with SEA tools (Xendit, Midtrans)? → Zoho, Salesforce

## Migration Checklist

Once you've chosen your CRM:

### Week 1: Setup
- [ ] Create account, add users
- [ ] Configure fields (contact, company, deal)
- [ ] Set up pipeline stages
- [ ] Integrate email (Gmail, Outlook)
- [ ] Connect calendar

### Week 2: Data Migration
- [ ] Export data from old system (CSV)
- [ ] Clean data (remove duplicates, fix formatting)
- [ ] Import to new CRM
- [ ] Verify data integrity (spot-check 50 records)

### Week 3: Training
- [ ] Admin training (2-4 hours)
- [ ] Sales team training (1-2 hours)
- [ ] Create cheat sheet (5-10 common tasks)
- [ ] Set up office hours (1 hour/day for questions)

### Week 4: Go Live
- [ ] Soft launch (pilot with 2-3 reps for 1 week)
- [ ] Fix issues, adjust workflows
- [ ] Full launch (all reps switch)
- [ ] Sunset old CRM

## FAQs

**Q: Can I try before I buy?**
A: Yes, all CRMs offer free trials:
- HubSpot: Free forever (basic features)
- Zoho: 15-day free trial
- Freshsales: 21-day free trial
- Pipedrive: 14-day free trial
- Salesforce: 30-day free trial

**Q: How long does implementation take?**
A:
- Pipedrive/Freshsales: 1-2 weeks
- HubSpot: 2-3 weeks
- Zoho: 3-4 weeks (more customization)
- Salesforce: 6-8 weeks (enterprise complexity)

**Q: Do I need a CRM consultant?**
A:
- HubSpot/Pipedrive/Freshsales: No (easy setup)
- Zoho: Optional (helpful for customization)
- Salesforce: Yes (highly recommended)

**Q: What if we outgrow our CRM?**
A: Most companies migrate CRMs every 3-5 years as they scale. Plan for this.

**Q: Can we integrate with our existing tools?**
A: Check the CRM's app marketplace:
- HubSpot: 1,000+ integrations
- Zoho: 500+ integrations
- Freshsales: 200+ integrations
- Pipedrive: 300+ integrations
- Salesforce: 5,000+ integrations
