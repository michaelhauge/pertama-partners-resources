## Detailed Comparison

### Mailchimp

**Best for**: Small businesses, beginners (1K-50K subscribers)

**Pricing** (10K subscribers):
- **Free**: Up to 500 subscribers, 1K sends/month
- **Essentials**: $13/mo (50K contacts) or $100/mo (10K)
- **Standard**: $20/mo or $200/mo (automation, A/B testing)
- **Premium**: $350/mo (advanced segmentation, phone support)

**Pros**:
- ✅ Easy to use (drag-and-drop builder)
- ✅ Free tier (good for testing)
- ✅ Templates (100+ pre-built)
- ✅ Integrations (300+)
- ✅ Landing pages included

**Cons**:
- ❌ Expensive at scale (>50K subscribers)
- ❌ Limited automation on lower tiers
- ❌ No phone support on Essentials
- ❌ Strict anti-spam policies (can suspend account)

**When to choose**: You're just starting out, <10K subscribers

---

### SendGrid

**Best for**: Transactional emails + marketing (developers)

**Pricing** (10K subscribers):
- **Free**: 100 emails/day (3K/month)
- **Essentials**: $20/mo (50K emails)
- **Pro**: $90/mo (1.5M emails)
- **Premier**: Custom (enterprise)

**Note**: SendGrid prices by emails sent, not subscribers

**Pros**:
- ✅ Developer-friendly (API-first)
- ✅ Transactional + marketing in one
- ✅ Cheapest for high volume
- ✅ Excellent deliverability
- ✅ Twilio-owned (reliable)

**Cons**:
- ❌ Less beginner-friendly (technical)
- ❌ Basic email builder (not drag-and-drop)
- ❌ Limited templates
- ❌ Weak automation (vs Klaviyo, HubSpot)

**When to choose**: You send transactional emails (receipts, password resets) + marketing

---

### ConvertKit

**Best for**: Creators, newsletters, course creators

**Pricing** (10K subscribers):
- **Free**: Up to 1K subscribers
- **Creator**: $29/mo (1K) or $59/mo (10K)
- **Creator Pro**: $59/mo (1K) or $119/mo (10K)

**Pros**:
- ✅ Simple (clean, minimal UI)
- ✅ Tag-based (vs lists - more flexible)
- ✅ Landing pages + forms included
- ✅ Great for newsletters
- ✅ Easy automation (visual builder)

**Cons**:
- ❌ Limited templates (basic designs)
- ❌ No e-commerce features
- ❌ Fewer integrations (vs Mailchimp)
- ❌ Not ideal for B2B marketing

**When to choose**: You're a creator (blogger, course creator, podcaster)

---

### Klaviyo

**Best for**: E-commerce (Shopify, WooCommerce)

**Pricing** (10K subscribers):
- **Email**: $150/mo (15K emails/month)
- **Email + SMS**: $175/mo (email + SMS)
- **Enterprise**: Custom

**Pros**:
- ✅ Best e-commerce integration (Shopify, WooCommerce)
- ✅ Advanced segmentation (purchase behavior)
- ✅ Predictive analytics (lifetime value, churn risk)
- ✅ Flows (abandoned cart, win-back, post-purchase)
- ✅ Strong ROI (customers see 30-50x ROI)

**Cons**:
- ❌ Expensive (3-5x Mailchimp)
- ❌ Overkill for non-e-commerce
- ❌ Steep learning curve
- ❌ Pricing can explode (scales with contacts)

**When to choose**: You're e-commerce, need advanced segmentation

---

### HubSpot

**Best for**: B2B marketing automation (lead nurturing)

**Pricing** (10K subscribers):
- **Free**: Basic email (2K sends/month)
- **Starter**: $20/mo (1K contacts)
- **Professional**: $800/mo (2K contacts) or $1,600/mo (10K)
- **Enterprise**: $3,200/mo (10K contacts)

**Pros**:
- ✅ Full marketing automation (workflows, lead scoring)
- ✅ CRM included (email + sales + service)
- ✅ Best for B2B (lead nurturing, ABM)
- ✅ Excellent reporting
- ✅ Integrates with everything

**Cons**:
- ❌ Very expensive (10x Mailchimp)
- ❌ Overkill for simple email
- ❌ Complex (steep learning curve)
- ❌ Vendor lock-in (hard to migrate out)

**When to choose**: You're B2B, need full marketing automation stack

- Good templates

## Implementation Checklist

**Week 1**: Setup
- [ ] Sign up for free trial
- [ ] Import contacts (CSV from old platform)
- [ ] Set up sender authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- [ ] Create 2-3 templates
- [ ] Test email (send to yourself)

**Week 2**: First Campaign
- [ ] Segment list (by interest, behavior)
- [ ] Write first email
- [ ] A/B test subject lines
- [ ] Schedule send
- [ ] Monitor results (open rate, click rate)

**Week 3**: Automation
- [ ] Set up welcome series (3-5 emails)
- [ ] Set up re-engagement campaign
- [ ] Test automation (trigger, flow, content)

**Week 4**: Optimization
- [ ] Review metrics (open rate, click rate, unsubscribe)
- [ ] Identify top-performing emails
- [ ] Adjust strategy for next month

## Email Marketing Best Practices

**Subject Lines**:
- Keep under 50 characters
- Personalize ("Hi [Name]")
- Create curiosity (not clickbait)
- A/B test everything

**Send Times** (SEA):
- B2B: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am SGT
- B2C: Weekend mornings, 8-10am local time
- E-commerce: Thursday-Friday (payday)

**Frequency**:
- Newsletter: 1x/week (max 2x)
- Promotional: 2-4x/month
- Transactional: As needed

**Unsubscribe Rate**:
- Target: <0.5% per campaign
- If >1%: Your content isn't resonating
