# Accounting Software for SEA (Comparison Guide)

**Purpose**: Choose accounting software for SEA business operations
**Time to Complete**: 2-3 hours (research + setup)
**Use Case**: Founders, CFOs, finance teams managing books in Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Philippines, Vietnam
**Output**: Accounting platform recommendation with setup plan

---

## Why Accounting Software Matters in SEA

**Local compliance is complex**:
- **Singapore**: GST reporting, IRAS filing, CPF payroll
- **Malaysia**: SST, EPF/SOCSO, EA forms
- **Indonesia**: VAT (PPN), e-Faktur, payroll (BPJS)
- **Thailand**: VAT, withholding tax, social security
- **Philippines**: BIR, VAT, withholding, SSS/PhilHealth/Pag-IBIG
- **Vietnam**: VAT, PIT, e-invoicing

**Manual accounting = risk**:
- Late filings → penalties
- Incorrect tax calculations → audits
- Missing records → compliance issues

**Right software = peace of mind**:
- Auto-calculate GST/VAT
- Generate tax reports
- Connect to banks (auto-reconciliation)
- Multi-currency support
- Payroll compliance

## Platform-by-Platform Comparison

### Xero

**Best For**: Singapore/Malaysia startups and SMEs

**Strengths**:
- ✅ **GST/SST compliant**: Auto-calculate GST (Singapore), SST (Malaysia)
- ✅ **Bank sync**: Auto-import transactions from DBS, OCBC, UOB, Maybank, CIMB
- ✅ **Multi-currency**: 160+ currencies
- ✅ **Invoicing**: Professional invoices, recurring billing
- ✅ **App ecosystem**: 1,000+ integrations (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
- ✅ **Mobile app**: iOS/Android

**Weaknesses**:
- ❌ **Limited SEA support**: No Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam compliance
- ❌ **Payroll add-on**: Extra cost ($10-20/month per employee)
- ❌ **Learning curve**: More complex than Wave

**Pricing** (Singapore):
- **Starter**: SGD 25/month (5 invoices, 1 user)
- **Standard**: SGD 46/month (unlimited invoices, 5 users)
- **Premium**: SGD 65/month (multi-currency, expenses, projects)

**Setup Time**: 2-4 hours (connect banks, import opening balances)

**Best For**:
- Singapore businesses with GST registration
- Malaysia businesses with SST
- SaaS companies with recurring billing
- 1-50 employees

**Not For**: Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam (no local compliance)

---

### QuickBooks Online

**Best For**: Multi-country businesses, US parent companies

**Strengths**:
- ✅ **Multi-country**: Singapore, Malaysia, US, UK, Canada, Australia
- ✅ **Multi-currency**: Auto FX rates, revaluation
- ✅ **Bank sync**: DBS, OCBC, UOB, Maybank, CIMB
- ✅ **Invoicing**: Recurring invoices, payment links
- ✅ **Payroll (SG)**: CPF, IRAS compliance (via QuickBooks Payroll)
- ✅ **Mobile app**: iOS/Android

**Weaknesses**:
- ❌ **More expensive**: $20-50/month (USD pricing)
- ❌ **US-centric**: Interface designed for US businesses
- ❌ **Limited Indonesia support**: No e-Faktur integration

**Pricing** (USD):
- **Simple Start**: $15/month (1 user, invoices, expenses)
- **Essentials**: $30/month (3 users, bills, time tracking)
- **Plus**: $45/month (5 users, inventory, projects)
- **Advanced**: $100/month (25 users, custom fields, batch invoices)

**Pricing** (Singapore - SGD):
- **Simple Start**: SGD 25/month
- **Essentials**: SGD 45/month
- **Plus**: SGD 70/month

**Setup Time**: 3-5 hours (more complex than Xero)

**Best For**:
- Multi-country operations (SG + US + UK)
- US parent company requiring QuickBooks
- Businesses with inventory
- 5-100 employees

**Not For**: Indonesia e-Faktur, Thailand/Vietnam compliance

---

### Wave

**Best For**: Freelancers, side projects, very small businesses

**Strengths**:
- ✅ **Free**: $0/month for core accounting
- ✅ **Simple**: Easiest to learn
- ✅ **Invoicing**: Unlimited invoices
- ✅ **Bank sync**: Auto-import transactions
- ✅ **Multi-currency**: Support for SGD, MYR, etc.

**Weaknesses**:
- ❌ **No GST/VAT automation**: Manual tax calculations
- ❌ **Limited SEA features**: No local compliance tools
- ❌ **Payments cost money**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (Wave Payments)
- ❌ **Basic reporting**: Limited customization

**Pricing**:
- **Accounting**: Free
- **Invoicing**: Free
- **Payments**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- **Payroll** (US only): $20-40/month (not available in SEA)

**Setup Time**: 1-2 hours (very simple)

**Best For**:
- Freelancers with <$50K revenue
- Side projects
- Solo founders
- Non-GST registered businesses

**Not For**: GST-registered businesses (too manual)

---

### MYOB

**Best For**: Singapore, Malaysia, Australia businesses with inventory

**Strengths**:
- ✅ **SEA focus**: Strong presence in SG, MY, AU
- ✅ **GST/SST compliant**: Auto-calculate GST (Singapore), SST (Malaysia)
- ✅ **Inventory**: Track stock, purchase orders, cost of goods
- ✅ **Payroll**: CPF (Singapore), EPF/SOCSO (Malaysia)
- ✅ **Multi-currency**: 160+ currencies
- ✅ **Local support**: Phone/email support in SEA timezone

**Weaknesses**:
- ❌ **Expensive**: SGD 50-150/month
- ❌ **Desktop-first**: Online version is newer, less mature
- ❌ **Old UI**: Interface feels dated compared to Xero

**Pricing** (Singapore):
- **MYOB Essentials**: SGD 50/month (5 users, invoicing, expenses)
- **MYOB AccountRight**: SGD 100/month (unlimited users, inventory, jobs)

**Setup Time**: 4-6 hours (more complex, inventory setup)

**Best For**:
- Singapore/Malaysia businesses with inventory
- Retail, wholesale, distribution
- 10-100 employees
- Businesses needing strong SEA support

**Not For**: Pure SaaS/service businesses (overkill)

- ✅ **Full ERP**: Accounting + CRM + inventory + HR
- ✅ **Multi-country**: Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Thailand, Philippines, Vietnam

- ✅ **Compliance**: Full VAT/GST/tax compliance for SEA

- ❌ **Very expensive**: $1,000-10,000/month (enterprise pricing)

**Setup Time**: 3-6 months (requires consultant)

**Best For**:
- Multi-country operations (5+ countries)
- Enterprise ($10M+ revenue)
- 100+ employees

**Not For**: Startups, SMEs (<$5M revenue)

## SEA-Specific Considerations

### Singapore

**Tax Requirements**:
- **GST**: 9% (as of 2024)
- **Filing**: Quarterly (e-filing via IRAS)
- **Payroll**: CPF (employer + employee contributions)

**Recommended**: Xero, QuickBooks, MYOB
**Avoid**: Wave (no GST automation)

---

### Malaysia

**Tax Requirements**:
- **SST**: 6% (Sales Tax) + 10% (Service Tax)
- **Filing**: Quarterly (MySST portal)
- **Payroll**: EPF, SOCSO, EIS, PCB (monthly)

**Recommended**: Xero, MYOB
**Avoid**: Wave (no SST automation)

---

### Indonesia

**Tax Requirements**:
- **PPN (VAT)**: 11%
- **e-Faktur**: Electronic invoicing (mandatory)
- **Payroll**: BPJS Kesehatan, BPJS Ketenagakerjaan

**Recommended**: NetSuite (only platform with full e-Faktur)
**Avoid**: Xero, QuickBooks (no e-Faktur support)

---

### Thailand

**Tax Requirements**:
- **VAT**: 7%
- **Withholding tax**: Various rates (1-10%)
- **Social security**: Monthly filing

**Recommended**: NetSuite
**Avoid**: Xero, QuickBooks (limited Thailand support)

---

### Philippines

**Tax Requirements**:
- **VAT**: 12%
- **BIR filing**: Multiple forms (2550M, 2551M, etc.)
- **Payroll**: SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG

**Recommended**: NetSuite
**Avoid**: Xero, QuickBooks (limited Philippines support)

---

### Vietnam

**Tax Requirements**:
- **VAT**: 10%
- **E-invoicing**: Mandatory for all businesses
- **PIT**: Personal income tax (monthly)

**Recommended**: NetSuite
**Avoid**: Xero, QuickBooks (no Vietnam support)

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake 1: Choosing Based on Price Alone

**Wrong**: "Wave is free, let's use it"
**Right**: "We're GST-registered, need Xero for compliance"

**Why**: Cheap/free software costs more in time and penalties if it doesn't handle compliance

---

### ❌ Mistake 2: Not Connecting Banks

**Wrong**: Manually entering every transaction
**Right**: Connect bank accounts, auto-import transactions

**Why**: Bank sync saves 5-10 hours/month, reduces errors

---

### ❌ Mistake 3: Ignoring Multi-Currency

**Wrong**: Record all transactions in SGD (even if invoice is in USD)
**Right**: Use multi-currency feature, track FX gains/losses

**Why**: Incorrect currency handling → tax reporting errors

---

### ❌ Mistake 4: Skipping Payroll Integration

**Wrong**: Use separate payroll tool (disconnected from accounting)
**Right**: Use integrated payroll (MYOB, NetSuite, or Xero add-on)

**Why**: Manual payroll entry → errors, missed CPF/EPF contributions
