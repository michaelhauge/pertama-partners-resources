# Social Media Setup (30 Days)

**Purpose**: Launch company social media presence from scratch
**Time Investment**: 20-30 hours over 30 days
**Team Size**: 1-2 people (founder or marketer)
**Output**: Active profiles, content calendar, first 1,000 followers

---

## Social Media Strategy

**Why Social Media?**
- **Brand awareness**: Reach potential customers where they already are
- **Thought leadership**: Share insights, build credibility
- **Customer support**: Answer questions, engage with customers
- **Hiring**: Attract talent, showcase culture
- **SEO**: Social signals help search rankings

**Reality Check**:
- Social media â‰  instant sales (long-term brand building)
- Requires consistency (3-5 posts/week minimum)
- Takes 6-12 months to see real ROI

**Theme**: Plan before you post

### Day 5-6: Define Content Pillars

**Content Pillars** = 3-5 topics you'll consistently post about

**Example** (B2B SaaS):
1. **Product Updates** (new features, improvements) - 30%
2. **Industry Insights** (trends, data, analysis) - 30%
3. **Customer Stories** (case studies, testimonials) - 20%
4. **Company Culture** (team, hiring, behind-the-scenes) - 10%
5. **Thought Leadership** (founder insights, hot takes) - 10%

**Content Format Mix**:
- **Text posts**: 40% (insights, tips, opinions)
- **Images**: 30% (quotes, infographics, product screenshots)
- **Videos**: 20% (demos, tutorials, behind-the-scenes)
- **Links**: 10% (blog posts, press coverage, resources)

**Deliverable**: Content pillars document (3-5 pillars with % allocation)

## Week 2: Content Creation (Days 8-14)

**Theme**: Create 2 weeks of content in advance

### Day 8-10: Write First 10 Posts

**Post Formula**:
1. **Hook** (first line grabs attention)
2. **Value** (insight, tip, story)
3. **CTA** (call to action: like, comment, share)

**Example Post** (LinkedIn):
"We analyzed 10,000 e-commerce stores and found 3 patterns in the top 1%:

1. Free shipping threshold at $50-75 (not $100+)
2. Product reviews >4.5 stars (social proof matters)
3. Checkout in <3 steps (reduce friction)

What's your conversion rate? Drop it in comments ðŸ‘‡"

**10 Post Ideas** (to start):

1. **Company launch**: "We're building [Product] to help [target audience] [solve problem]"
2. **Problem statement**: "[Stat]% of [audience] struggle with [problem]. Here's why..."
3. **Founder story**: "Why I started [Company]: [personal story]"
4. **Product demo**: "See [Product] in action (30-sec video)"
5. **Customer win**: "[Customer] increased [metric] by [%] using [Product]"
6. **Industry insight**: "The [industry] is changing. Here are 3 trends to watch..."
7. **Behind-the-scenes**: "Our team working on [feature]. Shipping next week!"
8. **Hiring**: "We're hiring [role]! Looking for [skills]. DM if interested."
9. **Milestone**: "We hit [metric]! Thank you to [audience] for support."
10. **Ask for feedback**: "Building [feature]. What would you want to see?"

**Deliverable**: 10 posts written (ready to schedule)

## Week 3: Engagement & Growth (Days 15-21)

**Theme**: Build audience, engage with community

### Day 15-16: Follow & Engage

**Follow Strategy**:
- [ ] Follow 50 target customers (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- [ ] Follow 20 industry leaders (thought leaders, influencers)
- [ ] Follow 10 competitors (learn from them)
- [ ] Follow 20 potential partners (complementary companies)

**Engagement Strategy** (15-30 min/day):
- [ ] Comment on 10 posts (add value, not spam)
- [ ] Like 20 posts (show support)
- [ ] Reply to comments on your posts (within 2 hours)
- [ ] DM 3-5 people (thank for follow, start conversation)

**Comment Formula**:
1. **Add value** (share insight, ask question)
2. **Be genuine** (no "Great post!")
3. **Tag others** (bring others into conversation)

**Example Comment**:
"Love this insight on [topic]. We saw similar results with [specific example]. Have you tried [approach]? Curious to hear your thoughts."

**Deliverable**: 100 new follows, 50+ engagements

---

## Social Media Metrics

**Vanity Metrics** (don't obsess):
- Follower count
- Likes

**Actionable Metrics** (focus on these):
- **Engagement rate**: (Likes + Comments + Shares) Ã· Impressions
- **Click-through rate**: Link clicks Ã· Impressions
- **Conversion rate**: Signups Ã· Link clicks
- **Follower growth rate**: (New followers - Unfollows) Ã· Total followers

**Target Benchmarks**:
- **Engagement rate**: 5-10% (B2B), 3-5% (B2C)
- **Follower growth**: 10-20% MoM (early days), 5-10% MoM (mature)

## Common Mistakes

### âŒ Mistake 1: Inconsistent Posting

**Wrong**: Post 10x one week, then ghost for 3 weeks
**Right**: Post 3-5x/week consistently

**Why**: Algorithm favors consistency. Followers forget you if you disappear.

---

### âŒ Mistake 2: Only Promotional Content

**Wrong**: Every post is "Buy our product!"
**Right**: 80% value, 20% promotion (80/20 rule)

**Why**: Nobody follows brands for ads. Provide value first.

---

### âŒ Mistake 3: No Engagement

**Wrong**: Post and ghost (never reply to comments)
**Right**: Reply within 2 hours (show you're listening)

**Why**: Social media = conversation, not broadcast.

---

### âŒ Mistake 4: Buying Followers

**Wrong**: Pay for 10,000 fake followers
**Right**: Grow organically (slow but real)

**Why**: Fake followers = low engagement = algorithm penalizes you.
