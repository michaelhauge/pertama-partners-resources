### SEA Currency Arbitrage Opportunities

**Major vendors with local currency pricing**:
- Salesforce: SGD, MYR, IDR, THB, PHP
- HubSpot: SGD, MYR
- Google Workspace: SGD, MYR, IDR, THB, VND, PHP
- Microsoft 365: SGD, MYR, IDR, THB, VND, PHP
- Zendesk: SGD, MYR
- Atlassian: SGD, MYR

**Typical savings**: 10-20% (local currency vs USD)

**Why vendors offer discounts in local currency**:
- Lower pricing power in emerging markets (ID, PH, VN)
- FX hedging (vendors lock in rates, pass savings to customers)
- Market penetration (lower prices to gain market share)
