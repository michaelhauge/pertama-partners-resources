## Communication Expectations

### Response Times

| Channel | Response Time | When to Use |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| **Slack DM** | Within 2 hours (during working hours) | Quick questions, urgent matters |
| **Slack @mention in channel** | Within 4 hours (during working hours) | Team discussions, input needed |
| **Slack channel message** | Within 24 hours | General team communication |
| **Email** | Within 24-48 hours | External communication, formal matters |
| **Phone call** | Immediate | True emergencies only |

**After hours**: No expectation to respond. Use "Do Not Disturb" freely.

### Meeting Culture
- **All meetings have**:
  - Calendar invite with clear title and agenda
  - Zoom/Meet link
  - Shared notes document (Notion/Google Docs)
  - Recording (optional, but recommended)

- **Default to async**: Can this be a Loom video or written doc instead?
- **Meeting-free days**: [e.g., "No meeting Thursdays" - optional]
- **No agenda = no meeting**: If organizer doesn't share agenda 24 hours before, attendees can decline

See detailed communication norms: [Link to CULTURE.md or internal handbook]

## Security & Data Protection

### Mandatory Security Measures

**All team members must**:
- ✅ Use [VPN tool] when on public WiFi or accessing company systems
- ✅ Use [password manager] for all work passwords
- ✅ Enable 2FA on all critical accounts (email, Slack, GitHub, AWS, etc.)
- ✅ Lock laptop when stepping away (auto-lock after 5 minutes)
- ✅ Use strong, unique passwords (no password reuse)
- ✅ Report suspicious emails/links to [#security Slack channel / security@company.com]

**For company-provided devices**:
- ✅ Install MDM profile (if applicable): [Link to IT setup guide]
- ✅ Keep OS updated (auto-updates enabled)
- ✅ Use antivirus/endpoint protection (if applicable)

### Lost or Stolen Devices
- Report immediately to [IT lead / manager]: [Contact info]
- Remote wipe will be initiated within 1 hour

### Prohibited Activities
- ❌ Storing company data on personal cloud storage (Dropbox, personal Google Drive)
- ❌ Sharing passwords with anyone (including coworkers)
- ❌ Using company equipment for illegal activities
- ❌ Downloading pirated software on company devices

See detailed security policy: [Link to security guide]
