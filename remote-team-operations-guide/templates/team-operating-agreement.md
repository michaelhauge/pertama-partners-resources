## Conflict Resolution

### When conflicts arise

**Step 1: Direct conversation** (person-to-person)
- Talk directly with the person (assume good intent)
- Use "I" statements ("I felt X when Y happened")
- Focus on behavior, not person

**Step 2: Escalate to manager** (if unresolved)
- If direct conversation doesn't work, involve manager
- Manager mediates conversation
- Aim for resolution, not blame

**Step 3: Escalate to HR** (if serious)
- For serious issues (harassment, discrimination, unethical behavior)
- Contact [HR contact]

**Conflict is normal**:
- Healthy teams have disagreements
- We disagree and commit (respectfully debate, then align on decision)
- No silent disagreements (speak up, don't stew)
