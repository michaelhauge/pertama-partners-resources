## How to Use This Checklist

1. **Rate each criterion** (0-5 scale):
   - 0 = Not available
   - 1-2 = Poor
   - 3 = Acceptable
   - 4-5 = Excellent

2. **Calculate weighted scores**:
   - Critical features: multiply score Ã— 3
   - Important features: multiply score Ã— 2
   - Nice-to-have features: multiply score Ã— 1

3. **Compare total scores** across tools

4. **Choose tool with highest score** for your needs
