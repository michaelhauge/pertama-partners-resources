## Email & Communication Management

### Use Case 1: Daily Email Triage

**Scenario**: You receive 100+ emails daily. Most are noise, some need immediate action.

**OpenClaw Solution**:
```
WhatsApp to OpenClaw:
"Summarize my emails from the last 4 hours.
Flag urgent ones and draft responses for the top 3."
```

**Result**:
- 5-minute summary instead of 30 minutes reading
- Never miss urgent requests
- Drafted responses ready to review and send

**Monthly Value**: 10 hours saved × $50/hr = $500

## Tips for Success

### Start with High-Value, Low-Risk Tasks

**Good first tasks**:
- ✅ Email summarization
- ✅ Document translation
- ✅ Research queries

**Avoid initially**:
- ❌ Automated financial transactions
- ❌ Customer-facing responses without review
- ❌ Sensitive data processing

---

### Build Trust Gradually

**Week 1**: Review every output
**Week 2**: Spot-check outputs
**Week 3**: Trust with monitoring
**Week 4**: Full automation

---

### Document Your Workflows

Create "playbooks" for your team:
```
Email Summary Playbook:
1. Send: "Summarize emails from [time period]"
2. Review: Check for urgent items
3. Act: Respond to flagged emails
4. Archive: File summary for reference
```
