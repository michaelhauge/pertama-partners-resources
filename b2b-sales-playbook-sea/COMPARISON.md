# AI Tools for B2B Sales: Comparison & Ratings

**Last Updated**: 2026-02-07
**Purpose**: Compare AI tools by capability for SEA B2B sales workflows

---

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Identify your workflow**: What do you need AI to do? (Research, proposals, calls, messaging)
2. **Check category ratings**: Each tool rated 1-5 stars for specific capabilities
3. **Consider budget**: Monthly costs for solo vs team
4. **Test before committing**: Most tools have free trials

**Budget ranges**:
- **Starter** ($20-40/mo): Solo founder, testing AI sales
- **Team** ($100-300/mo): 5-10 person sales team
- **Enterprise** ($500+/mo): Advanced features, high volume

## Category 1: General-Purpose AI (Research, Proposals, Messaging)

### Claude Pro

**Price**: $20/month
**Best for**: Proposals, research, long-form content, WhatsApp drafting

| Capability | Rating | Notes |
|------------|--------|-------|
| **Prospect research** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Best for deep company analysis, stakeholder mapping |
| **Proposal writing** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Excels at long-form content, maintains context well |
| **Email drafting** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Professional tone, concise |
| **WhatsApp drafting** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Great for short messaging (30-50 words) |
| **Discovery question gen** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Custom questions based on research |
| **Objection handling** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Contextual responses |
| **SEA cultural adaptation** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Adjusts tone well by country when prompted |
| **Call summarization** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Good if you paste transcription |
| **Data analysis** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Can analyze deal data, win/loss patterns |

**Strengths**:
- Longest context window (200K tokens) - paste entire discovery calls
- Best for long-form content (proposals 2,000+ words)
- Nuanced understanding of SEA cultures
- Handles complexity well (multi-step workflows)

**Weaknesses**:
- No built-in integrations (manual copy-paste)
- No image generation
- Can't browse web directly

**SEA-Specific**:
- Excellent for ID/MY/TH indirect communication styles
- Adapts tone well when given country context
- Good with Bahasa Indonesia, Thai, Vietnamese translation

**Cost**:
- Solo: $20/month
- Team (5 people): $100/month (shared prompts, individual accounts)

**Recommended for**:
✅ Proposal-heavy sales
✅ Complex B2B deals needing research
✅ WhatsApp/LINE messaging assistance
✅ SEA cultural adaptation

**Price**: $20/month
**Best for**: Quick responses, web research, image analysis

| Capability | Rating | Notes |
|------------|--------|-------|
| **Prospect research** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Good, can browse web for current info |
| **Proposal writing** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Solid, but less contextual than Claude for long content |

| **Call summarization** | ⭐⭐⭐ | Decent |
| **Image generation** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | DALL-E integration (Claude doesn't have this) |

- Solo: $20/month

✅ Image generation for presentations

## Category 2: Call Intelligence & Transcription

### Fireflies.ai

**Price**: $10-19/month (Pro), $39-99/month (Business for teams)
**Best for**: Call transcription, AI summaries, CRM integration

| Capability | Rating | Notes |
|------------|--------|-------|
| **Transcription accuracy** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 95%+ accuracy, handles SEA accents well |
| **AI call summary** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Excellent automatic summaries |
| **Action item extraction** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Good at identifying action items |
| **Speaker identification** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Accurate speaker labels |
| **CRM integration** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Integrates with Zoho, HubSpot, Salesforce |
| **Team collaboration** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Comment on transcripts, share clips |
| **Search across calls** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Search keywords across all calls |

**Strengths**:
- Best transcription quality (beats Otter, Zoom native)
- Handles SEA accents better than competitors
- Advanced AI features (sentiment analysis, topic detection)
- CRM auto-logging
- Searchable call library

**Weaknesses**:
- More expensive than Otter
- Overkill for solo founders (too many features)

**SEA-Specific**:
- Good with Singapore, Malaysian, Filipino English accents
- Handles code-switching (English + local language) reasonably

**Cost**:
- Solo: $10-19/month (Pro)
- Team (5 people): $195/month (Business)

**Recommended for**:
✅ Teams with >50 calls/month
✅ Need CRM integration
✅ Want searchable call library
✅ SEA English accents

## SEA-Specific Stack Recommendations

### Indonesia/Malaysia (WhatsApp-Heavy)

**Must-have**:
- Zoho CRM (WhatsApp Business API native)
- Claude Pro (WhatsApp message drafting)
- Fireflies.ai or Otter.ai (calls)

**Skip**:
- Email AI tools (Lavender) - WhatsApp is primary channel

**Cost**: $200-300/month for 5-person team

---

### Singapore (Email + WhatsApp Mix)

**Recommended**:
- Freshsales (better email AI) or Zoho (WhatsApp)
- Claude Pro (both email and WhatsApp)
- Fireflies.ai (many calls)

**Optional**:
- Lavender.ai (if heavy email outreach)

**Cost**: $250-350/month for 5-person team

---

### Thailand (LINE-Heavy)

**Recommended**:
- LINE Official Account (separate from this guide)
- Claude Pro (LINE message drafting)
- Zoho or Freshsales (CRM)
- Fireflies.ai (calls)

**Note**: No CRM has native LINE integration (manual logging needed)

**Cost**: $200-300/month for 5-person team

## Decision Matrix

Use this to choose your stack:

| If you... | Choose... | Skip... |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Sell primarily via WhatsApp (ID/MY) | Zoho CRM + Claude Pro | Email AI tools, non-WhatsApp CRMs |
| Heavy proposal writing | Claude Pro | ChatGPT (Claude better for long-form) |
| Need web research | ChatGPT Plus | Claude (no web browsing) |
| >50 discovery calls/month | Fireflies.ai | Otter (better AI), Zoom native |
| Budget <$50/month | Claude + Otter + Zoho Standard | Premium tools |
| Want best CRM AI | Freshsales (Freddy) | Basic CRMs |
| WhatsApp critical | Zoho CRM | HubSpot, Salesforce (weak WhatsApp) |
| Email-heavy (SG/PH) | Freshsales + Lavender | WhatsApp-focused tools |
| Testing AI workflows | Free tier: Claude Free, Zoom, manual | Paid tools initially |
