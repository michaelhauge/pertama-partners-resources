# AI-Powered Objection Handling

**Purpose**: Respond to 25 common sales objections with AI-generated, personalized responses in 30 seconds.

**How to use**:
1. Identify objection category (Price, Timing, Competition, etc.)
2. Copy AI prompt below
3. Feed prospect context (industry, pain point, your solution)
4. AI generates personalized response
5. Practice response before next call

---

## How to Use AI for Objection Handling

### Basic AI Prompt Template

```
I got this objection from a prospect: "[OBJECTION]"

Context:
- Company: [NAME]
- Industry: [e.g., fintech]
- Pain point: [e.g., 9-month sales cycles]
- Our solution: [e.g., AI-powered sales system]
- Our price: [e.g., $50K/year]
- Their current cost: [e.g., $1M/year in lost productivity]

Generate 3 response options:
1. Empathy + Reframe (address concern, show different perspective)
2. Question (uncover real objection behind stated objection)
3. Story/proof (share how similar company overcame same concern)

Keep each response under 100 words.
```

**Standard Response** (non-personalized):
"I understand price is a concern. Let me show you the ROI..."

**AI Prompt**:

## Pro Tips for Objection Handling with AI

### 1. Generate Multiple Response Options

**Instead of**:
```
Generate a response to: "Too expensive"
```

**Do this**:
```
Generate 3 response options to "Too expensive":
1. Empathy + Reframe (address concern, show value)
2. Question (uncover real objection)
3. Story (share similar customer experience)

Keep each under 75 words.
```

**Why**: Gives you options to choose the best fit for this prospect.

---

### 2. Practice Responses Out Loud

**After AI generates response**:
- Say it out loud 3 times
- Adjust awkward phrasing
- Make it sound like YOUR voice, not AI's voice

**Example**:
- AI: "I comprehend your concern about pricing."
- Your voice: "I hear you — price is a real concern."

---

### 3. Build an Objection Library

After each objection, save to your library:

**Objection**: "Too expensive"
**Context**: Fintech, $1M current cost, $50K our price
**Response used**: [paste response]
**Outcome**: Closed in 30 days

**Why**: Build pattern recognition. You'll see which responses work for which industries/personas.

---

### 4. Role-Play with AI

**Prompt**:
```
Let's role-play. You're a skeptical CFO at a fintech company. I'm selling you [SOLUTION]. Throw objections at me, and I'll practice responding. Give me feedback on my responses.
```

**Why**: Practice makes perfect. AI gives instant feedback on your responses.
