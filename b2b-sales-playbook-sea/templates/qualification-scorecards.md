# Qualification Scorecards (BANT-SEA & MEDDIC-SEA)

**Last Updated**: 2026-02-07
**Purpose**: Ready-to-use scoring sheets for qualifying SEA B2B deals

---

## How to Use These Scorecards

1. **After discovery call**: Score the prospect immediately (while fresh)
2. **Use AI to assist**: Feed call notes/transcription to AI for initial scoring
3. **Human final judgment**: Review and adjust AI scores
4. **Log in CRM**: Add scores to deal record
5. **Update as deal progresses**: Re-score after each major conversation

**Goal**: Consistent qualification across your sales team.

---

## BANT-SEA Scorecard

**Use for**: Most B2B deals, especially <$50K ACV, 3-6 month cycles

---

### Budget Score (1-5)

| Score | Criteria | Evidence | Red Flags |
|-------|----------|----------|-----------|
| **5** | Budget allocated, single approver, ready to buy | "We've set aside $X for this in Q2 budget" | None |
| **4** | Budget allocated, 2-3 approvers, 1-2 month timeline | "Finance approved the budget, just need CTO sign-off" | None |
| **3** | Budget available but not allocated, needs internal pitch | "We can find budget if the ROI is clear" | Competing priorities |
| **2** | Budget uncertain, would need to fight for it | "We'd need to see if we can get approval" | Multiple projects competing |
| **1** | No budget, "exploring options" | "No budget right now, maybe next year" | Disqualify |

**Your Score**: _____ / 5

**Evidence from call**:
```
[PASTE QUOTES OR NOTES ABOUT BUDGET DISCUSSION]
- Who controls budget?
- Approval process?
- Timeline for budget approval?
- Competing priorities?
```

**Red flags to watch**:
- Can't name who controls budget
- "We don't have budget right now"
- Layoffs or hiring freeze mentioned
- Multiple projects competing for same budget
