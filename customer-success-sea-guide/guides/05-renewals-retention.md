## 90-Day Renewal Preparation Timeline

### AI-Powered 90-Day Renewal Calendar

**Use this timeline for every renewal** (adjust for SEA cultural timing â€” see SEA section).

#### **DAY 90 (3 months before renewal date)**

**AI Task: Renewal Risk Scan**
- Run AI renewal risk detection (see prompt below)
- Identify at-risk renewals (health score <70, usage decline >20%, support issues)
- Prioritize by risk level (critical â†’ high â†’ medium â†’ low)

**CSM Action:**
- Review AI risk report
- Schedule internal planning meeting for critical/high risk accounts
- Flag expansion opportunities for healthy accounts (health score >85)

**Time**: 15 minutes (vs 2-3 hours manual)

---

#### **DAY 75 (2.5 months before)**

**AI Task: Value Quantification**
- Generate renewal value deck (ROI, usage stats, wins)
- Calculate value delivered vs subscription cost (ROI ratio)
- Identify 3 key wins to highlight in renewal meeting

**CSM Action:**
- Review AI-generated deck
- Add customer-specific context (quotes, anecdotes)
- Prepare expansion pitch if health score >85

**Time**: 20 minutes (vs 4-6 hours manual)

---

#### **DAY 60 (2 months before)**

**AI Task: Expansion Opportunity Analysis**
- Run expansion signal detection (from 04-expansion-upsell.md)
- Identify upsell/cross-sell opportunities
- Calculate expansion ARR potential

**CSM Action:**
- Review expansion opportunities
- Decide: renewal-only or renewal + expansion?
- If expansion: prepare tiered pricing options

**Time**: 15 minutes (vs 2-3 hours manual)

---

#### **DAY 50-55 (7-8 weeks before)**

**CSM Task: Proactive Renewal Meeting**
- Schedule renewal meeting (via WhatsApp/LINE in SEA, email in SG/PH)
- Present AI-generated value deck
- Address objections (use AI objection handler â€” see below)
- Discuss expansion if applicable

**Meeting agenda:**
1. Partnership recap (5 min)
2. Value delivered (10 min) â€” AI-generated ROI stats
3. Usage & adoption (5 min)
4. Renewal discussion (10 min)
5. Expansion opportunity (5 min, if applicable)
6. Next steps (5 min)

**Time**: 40-minute meeting + 20-minute prep

---

#### **DAY 45 (6 weeks before)**

**AI Task: Renewal Proposal Drafting**
- Generate renewal contract (same terms, price increase, or expansion)
- Calculate pricing (handle multi-currency for SEA)
- Draft email to send renewal proposal

**CSM Action:**
- Review AI-drafted proposal
- Send proposal via email + follow up on WhatsApp/LINE
- Set deadline for renewal decision (14-21 days in SEA, 7-10 days in SG/PH)

**Time**: 15 minutes (vs 2-3 hours manual)

---

#### **DAY 30 (1 month before)**

**CSM Task: Follow-Up & Negotiation**
- Check renewal status (pending, approved, objections?)
- If objections: use AI objection handler (see below)
- If approved: send to legal/finance for finalization
- If at-risk: escalate to manager + run save play

**Time**: 30 minutes per account

---

#### **DAY 14-21 (2-3 weeks before)**

**CSM Task: Finalization Push**
- For pending renewals: send reminder via WhatsApp/LINE
- For at-risk renewals: schedule executive-level call
- For approved renewals: finalize contract signature

**SEA-specific timing:**
- Malaysia/Indonesia/Thailand: Allow 21-30 days for decision (relationship cultures = slower)
- Singapore/Philippines: 14-21 days acceptable (more direct)
- Vietnam: 21 days (bureaucratic approval layers)

---

#### **DAY 7 (1 week before)**

**CSM Task: Last-Minute Escalation**
- For critical renewals not yet signed: executive escalation
- For healthy renewals: send friendly reminder
- Update renewal forecast in CRM

---

#### **RENEWAL DATE**

**CSM Task: Post-Renewal Actions**
- If renewed: celebrate with customer (thank you message)
- If expanded: hand off to implementation team
- If churned: conduct exit interview (AI can analyze exit themes)
- Update CRM (renewal status, ARR change, churn reason if applicable)
