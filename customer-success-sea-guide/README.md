## The Problem

Customer success in Southeast Asia faces unique challenges:

- ‚ùå **Customers churn silently** ‚Äî By the time you notice, it's too late (relationship cultures avoid direct confrontation)
- ‚ùå **Health scoring is reactive** ‚Äî Traditional metrics (NPS, login counts) miss the real warning signs
- ‚ùå **Onboarding is generic** ‚Äî One-size-fits-all checklists don't match customer context or SEA business culture
- ‚ùå **Expansion opportunities get missed** ‚Äî Without dedicated CS teams, upsell signals go unnoticed
- ‚ùå **Renewals are last-minute scrambles** ‚Äî No systematic approach to relationship nurturing and value reinforcement
- ‚ùå **QBRs take days to prepare** ‚Äî And still feel generic without the time to personalize

**Traditional CS training assumes**:
- You have a dedicated CS platform ($30K+/year)
- You have a full CS team with bandwidth
- Your customers communicate directly when unhappy
- Email and phone are your primary CS channels

**This playbook is different**:
- AI handles 70% of CS workflows (health scoring, QBR prep, communication drafting)
- Built for small teams (1-5 people) managing 20-200 accounts
- Adapted for SEA relationship cultures (indirect communication, WhatsApp/LINE-heavy)
- Tools cost $20-150/month (not $30K/year platforms)

Traditional CS approaches don't scale for small teams in SEA markets. Here's the transformation:
