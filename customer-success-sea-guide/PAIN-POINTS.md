## Multiple High Scores? Priority Framework

If you scored HIGH (15-25 points) in multiple sections, use this framework:

### 1. Start with Health Monitoring IF:
- You have unexpected churn (customers you thought were healthy churn)
- You're flying blind on customer health
- You have 50+ accounts and can't manually track everyone

**Why first**: Health scoring is the foundation. You can't prevent churn, drive expansion, or manage renewals if you don't know who's at risk.

---

### 2. Start with Churn Prevention IF:
- You're losing customers regularly (>10% annual churn)
- Churn is your #1 revenue problem right now
- You have SEA customers who go silent before churning

**Why first**: Stopping the bleeding is more urgent than growth. Retained customers = foundation for expansion.

---

### 3. Start with Onboarding IF:
- New customers are churning during/shortly after onboarding
- Time-to-value is 60+ days (should be 30 days or less)
- Activation rates are below 60%

**Why first**: Fix the top of the funnel before optimizing retention/expansion. New customers who don't activate never reach renewal.

---

### 4. Start with Expansion IF:
- Retention is solid (churn <5%) but growth is flat
- Customers are outgrowing your current offering
- You see power users but can't systematically convert â†’ revenue

**Why first**: You've proven retention, now unlock growth. Expansion revenue is the highest-margin revenue.

---

### 5. Start with Renewals IF:
- Renewal timing is the main issue (not churn itself)
- You have complex multi-stakeholder renewals
- Renewals are unpredictable (60-day surprises)

**Why first**: If renewals are stressful but not failing (yet), systematize them before they become a retention problem.

## Pain Point Details: What Each Score Means

### Onboarding Pain Points

**A1: Customers take 30+ days to see value**
- **Symptom**: Low activation rates, customers don't use product fully
- **Root Cause**: Generic onboarding that doesn't match use case
- **AI Solution**: Personalized 30-60-90 day plans based on customer goals

**A2: Generic onboarding checklists**
- **Symptom**: One-size-fits-all approach, customers confused about next steps
- **Root Cause**: No time to customize for each customer
- **AI Solution**: AI generates use-case-specific onboarding flows

**A3: Kickoff prep takes 2+ hours**
- **Symptom**: CS team spends hours researching company, building deck
- **Root Cause**: Manual research, starting from scratch each time
- **AI Solution**: AI kickoff deck generation from CRM + sales notes

**A4: Lost track of milestones**
- **Symptom**: Follow-ups slip, customers fall through cracks
- **Root Cause**: No systematic tracking system
- **AI Solution**: AI milestone tracking + auto-drafted check-ins

**A5: Customers go dark during onboarding**
- **Symptom**: Weeks without engagement, delayed activation
- **Root Cause**: Not enough touchpoints or wrong touchpoints
- **AI Solution**: AI-triggered engagement based on activity patterns

---

### Health Monitoring Pain Points

**B1: Don't know who's at risk until they threaten to churn**
- **Symptom**: Reactive firefighting, surprises
- **Root Cause**: No early warning system
- **AI Solution**: Predictive health from leading indicators

**B2: Lagging indicators only**
- **Symptom**: NPS/ticket data = already churning
- **Root Cause**: Backward-looking metrics
- **AI Solution**: AI analyzes usage patterns, stakeholder changes, sentiment

**B3: "Green" accounts churn unexpectedly**
- **Symptom**: Accounts scored healthy suddenly churn
- **Root Cause**: Missing hidden risk signals
- **AI Solution**: AI identifies non-obvious patterns (exec turnover, feature abandonment)

**B4: Manual, out-of-date scores**
- **Symptom**: Health scores weeks old, decisions on stale data
- **Root Cause**: Manual updates don't scale
- **AI Solution**: Automated weekly health refresh

**B5: Data exists but no pattern recognition**
- **Symptom**: Product analytics available but not actionable
- **Root Cause**: No system to translate data â†’ health
- **AI Solution**: AI health models from usage, support, engagement

---

### Churn & Retention Pain Points

**C1: Customers churn without warning**
- **Symptom**: No chance to save, learn about churn from cancellation notice
- **Root Cause**: Missed early signals or customers don't vocalize
- **AI Solution**: AI predicts churn 60-90 days ahead

**C2: React too late**
- **Symptom**: By the time you notice, decision is made
- **Root Cause**: Warning signals come too late in traditional CS
- **AI Solution**: AI identifies leading indicators (usage drop, sentiment shift)

**C3: Generic save strategies**
- **Symptom**: "What can we do to keep you?" feels desperate
- **Root Cause**: No framework for personalized interventions
- **AI Solution**: AI drafts save strategies based on churn driver

**C4: SEA customers go silent**
- **Symptom**: In MY/ID/TH, customers avoid confrontation, just churn quietly
- **Root Cause**: Cultural communication styles (indirect)
- **AI Solution**: AI detects behavioral signals (not verbal feedback)

**C5: No intervention playbooks**
- **Symptom**: Inconsistent response to churn risk
- **Root Cause**: Ad hoc approach, no systematized process
- **AI Solution**: AI-triggered playbooks based on risk type

---

### Expansion & Growth Pain Points

**D1: Missing upsell/cross-sell opportunities**
- **Symptom**: Customers outgrow you and leave instead of upgrading
- **Root Cause**: No system to identify expansion readiness
- **AI Solution**: AI flags power users, feature gaps, timing signals

**D2: Reactive expansion**
- **Symptom**: Wait for customer to ask "What else do you have?"
- **Root Cause**: No proactive expansion program
- **AI Solution**: AI identifies expansion triggers from usage

**D3: Can't identify ready accounts**
- **Symptom**: Pitch everyone or no one, not strategic
- **Root Cause**: No criteria for expansion readiness
- **AI Solution**: AI scoring for expansion potential + timing

**D4: Quota-driven, not value-driven**
- **Symptom**: Expansion feels pushy, damages relationship
- **Root Cause**: Sales/CS incentives vs customer value
- **AI Solution**: AI recommends timing based on customer milestones

**D5: Power users don't convert to revenue**
- **Symptom**: High usage but no expansion revenue
- **Root Cause**: No systematic conversion workflow
- **AI Solution**: AI identifies power users + drafts value-based proposals

---

### Renewals & QBR Pain Points

**E1: Last-minute renewals**
- **Symptom**: 60-day or less lead time, scrambling
- **Root Cause**: No systematic renewal prep workflow
- **AI Solution**: 120-day renewal workflow with milestones

**E2: Generic renewal quotes**
- **Symptom**: "Here's your quote" email, no value reinforcement
- **Root Cause**: No bandwidth to personalize
- **AI Solution**: AI-drafted value summaries from usage data

**E3: QBRs take 4-6 hours to prep**
- **Symptom**: QBRs skipped or templated due to time
- **Root Cause**: Manual deck building from scattered data
- **AI Solution**: AI generates QBR decks from analytics + transcripts

**E4: Missing decision-makers**
- **Symptom**: Pitch to wrong stakeholders, renewal stalls
- **Root Cause**: No systematic stakeholder mapping
- **AI Solution**: AI maps decision-makers from LinkedIn + org changes

**E5: Awkward pricing conversations**
- **Symptom**: Price increases feel arbitrary or defensive
- **Root Cause**: No framework for value-based pricing
- **AI Solution**: AI-recommended pricing based on value delivered

## Still Not Sure? Common Situations

### "All my scores are LOW (0-10 per section)"
**What this means**: CS is working well, or you have <10 accounts

**Recommendation**:
- If <10 accounts: Manual CS is fine, revisit when you hit 20-30 accounts
- If 10-50 accounts: Your CS is strong! Focus on growth (sales, marketing)
- Consider [Expansion & Upsell](guides/04-expansion-upsell.md) to unlock revenue from happy customers

---

### "All my scores are HIGH (15+ per section)"
**What this means**: CS is overwhelmed, systemic issues

**Recommendation**:
- **Immediate**: Start with Health Monitoring (foundation for everything else)
- **Week 2**: Add Churn Prevention (stop the bleeding)
- **Week 4**: Add Onboarding (fix the top of funnel)
- This is a 12-week full transformation, not a quick fix

---

### "My team won't adopt AI tools"
**What this means**: Change management challenge

**Recommendation**:
- Start with one workflow that has a visible time-saving win (QBR prep)
- Let one person pilot, show results to team
- See [FAQ: Team Adoption](FAQ.md#team-adoption) for strategies

---

### "I don't have budget for any AI tools"
**What this means**: Resource-constrained

**Recommendation**:
- Start with Claude Free (limited but functional)
- Focus on highest-ROI workflow: Health Monitoring or Churn Prevention
- Calculate retained revenue from one saved customer = pays for tools 10-50x over

---

### "We operate in markets outside SEA"
**What this means**: Country-specific guidance won't all apply

**Recommendation**:
- Core AI workflows apply globally (health scoring, QBRs, etc.)
- Skip [CULTURAL-CS.md](CULTURAL-CS.md) or adapt principles to your markets
